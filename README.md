# 📊 Task 1: Bank Marketing Prediction

## 📌 Problem Statement
The objective of this project is to predict whether a bank customer will subscribe to a term deposit based on marketing campaign data.

## ⚙️ Model Used
- Random Forest Classifier

## 📊 Steps Performed
- Data preprocessing
- Handling categorical variables (Label Encoding)
- Train-test split
- Model training
- Model evaluation
- SHAP analysis for explainability

## 📈 Results
The model achieved good accuracy in predicting customer responses.

# 👥 Task 2: Customer Segmentation

## 📌 Problem Statement
The goal is to segment customers based on their annual income and spending score.

## ⚙️ Model Used
- K-Means Clustering

## 📊 Steps Performed
- Data loading and cleaning
- Feature selection
- Applying K-Means clustering
- Visualization of clusters

## 📈 Insights
- High income & high spending → VIP customers  
- Low income & low spending → Budget customers  
- High income & low spending → Careful customers  
- Low income & high spending → Impulsive buyers  
- Medium income & spending → Average customers
